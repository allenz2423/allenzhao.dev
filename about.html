About Me

I born and raised in Brooklyn, New York. I currently attends the Highschool of Telecommuncation Arts and Technologies. I have a couple hobbies consisting of keyboard building, computer building, and experimenting with audio equipment. For me, good equipment can make or break a setup. My philosophy on picking out a setup is simple. Everything has to be perfectly balanced. I could have the best headphones in the world, but if I don't have the DAC or amp to run it, it wouldn't be performing at it's peak. I won't be getting the full potential of that pair of headphones. Same applies with my life. I try to regulate my time, but if something comes to mind and I don't have more important matters to attend to, then I follow decide to do what I want to do. If I start a project, I promise to myself that I would finish it. Breaking promises isn't really my style.
